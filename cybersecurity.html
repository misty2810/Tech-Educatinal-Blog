Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, damage, or theft. In today's digital world, where almost every aspect of our lives is connected online, cybersecurity is critical to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive information. It involves various strategies, tools, and measures to defend against cyber threats such as hacking, malware, phishing, and data breaches.

Applications of Cybersecurity

  • Data Protection and Privacy
  • Network Security
  • Application Security
  • Identity and Access Management (IAM)

Learning Cybersecurity

To start learning cybersecurity, begin by gaining a solid understanding of networking concepts, operating systems, and protocols, as these form the foundation of security. Then, explore online courses, certifications like CompTIA Security+, and hands-on labs to build practical skills in areas like ethical hacking, network defense, and incident response.
