Data-science

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that combines statistics, mathematics, programming, and domain knowledge to extract valuable insights and knowledge from structured and unstructured data.

Applications of Data-science

  • Predictive Analytics
  • Healthcare and Medical Research
  • Fraud Detection and Prevention
  • Recommendation Systems

Learning Data-science

To start learning data science, begin by mastering the basics of programming languages like Python or R, along with essential mathematics and statistics. Then, explore online courses, tutorials, and practice working with datasets to gain hands-on experience in data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization.
